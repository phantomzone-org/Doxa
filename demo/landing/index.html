Tessera private transfer demo

About Tessera

Tessera sits as a smart contract with private states on a public blockchain and provides compliant privacy for digital assets workflows (transfers, issuance, etc.) of institutions and their end-users. Tessera is non-custodial, does not introduce any additional trust-intermediary, and provides privacy for both inter-institution and intra-institution flows. Tessera maintains strong data silos across institutions. For more information on Tessera, we refer to the following link .

Demo

The private transfer demo has 3 entities (3 subpools) — Entity A, Entity B, Entity C. Users can create an account inside the subpool of any entity via their client wallet interface. Each entity has its respective admin dashboard via which it configures its subpool and views activities within it. At all times, the entity has access to all information necessary to comply with regulations in its local jurisdiction. Private transfers both within a subpool and across subpools are private. This can be confirmed by monitoring the Tessera smart contract.
